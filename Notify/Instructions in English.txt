Instructions from the Wikoristan supplement
To create a guess, click on the CREATE button and in the window that opens, set the title and save
To edit your guess, you need to click on the EDIT button and change the data in the window that opens, you must lose the Title and select the hour
To see a fortune-telling, you need to click on the empty cell in front of the fortune-telling and then click the VIEW button and the fortune-telling will be deleted
To change your language, click on the button tune up and then CHANGE MOVIE and save
To change the autostart status, click on the setup button AUTOSTART and save
To change the sound of the prediction, click on the BROWSE settings button, select a file with sound in the .wav format and save
